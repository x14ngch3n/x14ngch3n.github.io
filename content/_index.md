## [Biography](#biography)

I am a second-year PhD student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, supervised by [Charles Zhang](https://cse.hkust.edu.hk/~charlesz).
Before that, I obtained my Bachelor's (2021) and Master's (2024) degrees at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

Currently my research interests focus on [Binary Lifting](https://decompilation.wiki/fundamentals/cfg-recovery/lifting/#llvm-ir-based-lifting) (Binary -> Assembly -> EIR -> HIR) and its downstream applications (e.g., bug detection, understanding, agent harnessing).
I am actively involved in the [Clearblue](https://clearblueinnovations.org/) research project where I work around a [LLVM-based binary lifter](https://dl.acm.org/doi/abs/10.1145/3620665.3640382).

In my spare time, I enjoy playing basketball, video gaming, [hiking](https://maps.app.goo.gl/TiUNYrxkEL8AH1ZYA), and cooking.
